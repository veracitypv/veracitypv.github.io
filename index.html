About

Vera has over 20 years experience in pharmacovigilance and drug safety. She has worked in large multinational companies and start-up biotech companies with “IND to NDA” experience across most therapeutic areas and modalities. Her expertise ranges from pharmacovigilance operations to signal detection. She has created systems and processes to ensure inspection readiness and regulatory compliance. Her motto is: “ensuring drug safety is not a choice but a philosophy“.
